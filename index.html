Who We Are

SurakshaKavach is more than just a platform; it's a community-driven initiative aimed at creating a safer society. Our tools and resources are designed to empower individuals and authorities to work together in crime prevention and resolution.

Our commitment to safety is unwavering. We believe in:

  • Prompt and effective crime reporting mechanisms.
  • Strengthening community-law enforcement relationships.
  • Providing educational resources for crime prevention
  • Emergency assistance when you need it most
  • Enhancing Technological Integration for Crime Tracking and Analysis.
  • Fostering a Culture of Mutual Respect and Cooperation
